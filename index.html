My D&D characters

Aust
Half elf paladin of the Silver Flame.
He has taken an oath to help the innocent and smite the evil.
Aust has funded (by accident) a fashion workshop called Holy Fashion where he usually helps when he's not on the battlefield.

Aust (Alternative timeline)
One day Aust and the rest of his team got an invitation to a free vacation.
On this timeline Aust is barely holding on to his sanity and oath.

Thokk
Thokk is a half orc barbarian who excels at fighting and is always ready to fight everything that crosses his path.
Thokk is always the first one to run into a fight disregarding stealth and defence.
Because of this, Thokk is bad at just about everything that isn't related to fighting.
