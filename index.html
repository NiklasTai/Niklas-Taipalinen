My D&D characters

Thokk
Thokk is a half orc barbarian who excels at fighting and is always ready to fight everything that crosses his path.
Thokk is always the first one to run into a fight disregarding stealth and defence.
Because of this, Thokk is bad at just about everything that isn't related to fighting.
